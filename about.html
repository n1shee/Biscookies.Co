Background of The Business

Biscookies was formely a small business owned by a few students graduated from UiTM. The purpose of this business is to spread the happiness that people feel during festive season such as Hari Raya, Christmas, Deepavali and many more! From there, Biscookies grew a lot and able to have their own bakery and cafe to serve their customer.

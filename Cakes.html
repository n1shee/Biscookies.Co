CAKES

A THEMED CAKES FOR EVERY OCASSION

Baking a cake is a common way to bring out the sweetness in a dish. Cakes have evolved from their original bread-based ancestry into a broad category of sweet treats. Flour, sugar, eggs, butter or oil, a liquid, and leavening agents are the standard cake components. Many variations are possible by changing the base components and adding or subtracting things like fruit (dried, candied, or fresh), nuts, chocolate, and extracts (vanilla, almond, etc.). Cakes may also be frosted with butter cream or another frosting, filled with fruit preserves or dessert sauces (such pastry cream), and embellished with marzipan, piped borders, or candied fruit. Cake is a common celebration food for events like birthday parties, anniversaries, and weddings.

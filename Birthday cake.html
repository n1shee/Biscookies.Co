BEST BIRTHDAY CAKE FLAVOUR COMBINATIONS

A birthday cake is a special kind of cake often served during birthday parties. Birthday cakes are often multi-layer confections covered with icing and topped with lighted candles in the shape of the celebrant's age. Different forms include cupcakes, cake pops, pastries, and tarts. Greetings ("Happy birthday") and the honoree's name are common additions to the cake.
